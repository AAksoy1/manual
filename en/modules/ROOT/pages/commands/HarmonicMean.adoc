:page-en: commands/HarmonicMean

HarmonicMean( <List of Numbers> )::
  Returns the https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Harmonic_mean[harmonic mean] of given list of numbers.

[EXAMPLE]
====

`++HarmonicMean({13, 7, 26, 5, 19})++` yields _9.79_.

====
