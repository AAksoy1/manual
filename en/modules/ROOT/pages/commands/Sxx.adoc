:page-en: commands/Sxx

Sxx( <List of Numbers> )::
  Calculates the statistic stem:[\sum x^2 - \frac{(\sum x)^2}\{n}].

Sxx( <List of Points> )::
  Calculates the statistic stem:[\sum x^2 - \frac{(\sum x)^2}\{n}] using the _x_-coordinates of the given points.
