AffineRatio( <Point A>, <Point B>, <Point C> )::
  Returns the affine ratio _λ_ of three collinear points _A_, _B_ and _C_, where _C = A + λ * AB_.

[EXAMPLE]
====

`++AffineRatio((-1, 1), (1, 1), (4, 1))++` yields _2.5_

====
