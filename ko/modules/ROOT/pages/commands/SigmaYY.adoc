:page-en: commands/SigmaYY

SigmaYY( <List of Points> )::
  Calculates the sum of squares of _y_-coordinates of the given points.

[EXAMPLE]
====

Let `++list = {(-3, 4), (-1, 4), (-2, 3), (1, 3), (2, 2), (1, 5)}++` be a list of points. `++SigmaYY(list)++` yields _a
= 79_.
