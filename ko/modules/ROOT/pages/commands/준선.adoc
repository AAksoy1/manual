Directrix( <Conic> )::
  Yields the directrix of the conic.

[EXAMPLE]
====

`++Directrix(x^2 - 3x + 3y = 9)++` yields the line _y_ = 4.5

====

[NOTE]
====

See also the xref:/s_index_php?title=Focus_Command_action=edit_redlink=1.adoc[Focus] command.

====
