CircularArc[ <Midpoint>, <Point>, <Point> ]::
CircularArc[Point M, Point A, Point B]::
  Creates a circular arc with midpoint _M_ between points _A_ and _B_.

[NOTE]
====
Point _B_ does not have to lie on the arc.
